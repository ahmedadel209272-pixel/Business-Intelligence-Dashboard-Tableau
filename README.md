## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of BlueRidge Event Production's business performance across four key areas: executive KPIs, customer insights, product performance, and supplier analysis.

### Key Metrics at a Glance
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| 💰 Total Sales | $1,282,144 |
| 📈 Total Profit | $640,170 |
| 👥 No. of Customers | 150 |
| 📦 Total Items Sold | 33,769 |
| 🛒 No. of Orders | 6,135 |

---

## 📄 Dashboard Pages

### 1. 🏢 Executive Overview
- Total revenue, profit, and order trends over time
- Geographic sales breakdown by region
- Monthly profit performance

### 2. 👥 Customer Insights
- Top customers by revenue
- Sales breakdown by age group and gender
- City-level customer mapping

### 3. 📦 Products Performance
- Revenue by product category (Electronics, Home Appliances, Hardware, etc.)
- Top product pricing comparison
- Total sales and revenue per product

### 4. 🤝 Suppliers Overview
- Profit contribution by supplier
- Customer volume per supplier
- Regional market share analysis

---

## ⚙️ Features

- ✅ Interactive filters (Age Group, City, Supplier Name, Top N)
- ✅ Dynamic bar charts and column charts
- ✅ Geographic map visualizations
- ✅ KPI summary cards
- ✅ Price range slider filter
